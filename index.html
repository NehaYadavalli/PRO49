Here are some origami designs.

Chameleon Origami-this is a versatile one. You can do it however you like. Any color would work, as these lizards change colors!

    Chameleons come in many sizes and shapes. Make your own custom chameleon!

Bear Origami turns a fierce and dangerous animal into a cute and docile paper creature!

    Bears are extraordinarily intelligent creatures. They can feel deep emotions, just like humans!

Panda Origami is special because all of the black and white papers fold perfectly to symbolize realistic color patterns of an actual panda!

    Pandas are adorable animals who spend most of their days eating. Bamboo is their main diet!
